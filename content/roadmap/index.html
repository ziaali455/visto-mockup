Your Journey

The Road to Your VistoPay Card

From eligibility check to your first swipe, here's what happens next.

Step 1 - Complete

Eligibility Assessment

You've completed the visa eligibility survey. Your profile has been evaluated against B1/B2 visa requirements including ties to home country, travel history, and financial readiness.

Step 2 - In Progress

Document Preparation

Gather your passport, financial documents, employment letters, and travel itinerary. Our checklist ensures you have everything needed for a strong application.

Step 3 - Upcoming

Visa Interview Prep

Access our interview preparation guides, common questions, and tips from successful applicants. Practice with our mock interview simulator.

Step 4

Visa Approval & Card Setup

Once your visa is approved, we'll immediately begin setting up your VistoPay debit card. Provide your US address and we'll ship your card.

Step 5

Activate & Start Spending

Activate your card through the VistoPay app. Get an instant virtual card for online purchases while your physical card is in transit. Pay rent, bills, and everyday expenses with zero hassle.

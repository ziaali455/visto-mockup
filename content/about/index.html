About Us

Financial access for every immigrant

VistoPay was born from a simple frustration: why is it so hard for immigrants to get basic financial services in the US? No credit history, no Social Security number, no account. We're changing that.

We combine visa eligibility assessment with instant financial access. Whether you're a student paying rent, a business traveler covering expenses, or a visitor exploring the country - VistoPay has your back.

  • ✓ No US credit history required
  • ✓ Works with B1 and B2 visa holders
  • ✓ FDIC-insured accounts
  • ✓ Instant virtual card on approval
  • ✓ Support in 12+ languages
